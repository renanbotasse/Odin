Odin Recipes: Small Barley Cakes

Ingredients

  • Barley flour
  • Water or milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with water or milk and some salt.
  2. The dough should be kneaded for a long time.
  3. into small balls that are squished flat and thin
  4. Baked on top of clay shards or the like which are placed upon the embers
